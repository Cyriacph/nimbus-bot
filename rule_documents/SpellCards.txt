For an easy, tactile way to track actions, give 1
set of 3 cards to each hero. Whenever heroes
roll Initiative, they’ll draw 1, 2, or 3 cards
(depending on their roll) to represent their
actions. They’ll discard an action card whenever they spend an action, and when their turn
is over, draw 3 cards again.

For more tactical depth, heroes can use
the back faces of their cards instead. Each has
1 boon, 1 bane, and 1 neutral card, adding
another layer of strategy for their turns. If you
have any extra cards, a hero could shuffle 2
decks together for even more variability!
Note. Heroes can use either KEY stat for the
cards, but must use the same number for both
the boon and bane cards. For abilities that costs
more than 1 action, apply both card effects.
